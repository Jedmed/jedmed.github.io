How to Play

Two players compete to harvest their crops on the same field. Each harvested crop is worth 1 point. The first player to 10 points wins!

What Can I Do?

Each player may only take one action on their turn:

  • Plant a Seed:Click on an empty field to plant a seed
  • Remove Weeds:A seed can not be planted on an area with weeds, click on a weed to remove it
  • Water Plants:A seed needs water in order to grow into a plant, select the WATER PLANT and splash your water over an area in order for the seeds to grow! You immediately get points for any plant
    WARNING! Watering seeds will grow plants for both players!
